To make use of the application, you just have to clone the repository on your machine and then open it with Visual Studio with the NET 6.0 framework. When the process has finished, the execution can start. In case you want to perform the validation of the test units, you must go to the "Solutions Explorer" tab and there, right-click on the TestUnitsThales project and click on the "Run Tests" option, clarifying that the two projects have to be running at the same time
